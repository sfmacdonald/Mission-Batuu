EPISODE XII
Journey To Batuu


Intrepid Galactic Explorers, A unique and exciting adventure awaits you in a distant galaxy far, far away. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, will transport you to the planet of Batuu, located on the outer rim of the Star Wars Galaxy, right within the magical realms of Disneyland's Galaxy's Edge.

As newly appointed members of the Resistance, your journey will be filled with challenges and mysteries to solve. You will navigate through the bustling Black Spire Outpost which is currently hosting a celebration, encountering alien species, droids, and perhaps even cross paths with some legendary heroes and villains of the galaxy.

Your mission objectives are as follows:

Pilot the Millennium Falcon: You will take control of the fastest ship in the galaxy (which made the Kessel Run in 12 parcecs) on a special mission. Each of you will play a critical role in its success, whether as pilots, gunners, or engineers.

Join the Rise of the Resistance: Embark on a daring adventure to face the First Order and help the Resistance in a climactic battle. Be brave, as the fate of the galaxy rests in your hands.

Explore the Outpost: Your skills as explorers will be tested as you navigate through the markets, shops, and hidden alleys of Batuu. Keep your eyes open for unique treasures and galactic delicacies that you can bring back from your journey.

Encounter the Inhabitants: Engage with the locals, traders, and maybe even some undercover Resistance fighters. Each interaction may reveal secrets or tasks that will aid you in your mission.

Be mindful of your surroundings as it is believed that Captain Phasma and her First Order stormtroopers are in the area. Rendevous with Resistance Hero & Jedi Rey Skywalker at the docking bay where the Millenium Falcon is stationed. Also, other Jedi are known to be at the outpost and will assist in honing your lightsaber skills.

Dress appopriately to blend in with the crowd. Do not forget also to nourish your body with Blue Milk and other galactic goodies for they will aid you on your journey.

Remember, the force will be your guide. It's up to you to use it wisely, work together as a team, and most importantly, have fun creating unforgettable memories in this extraordinary adventure.

May the Force be with you, always. Love,

Senator Lapham & Jedi Master MacDonald
